Advanced Walking Dynamics

Overview

Advanced walking dynamics explores the complex interactions between the Duck Droid's mechanical structure and its environment. Key aspects include:

  • Dynamic balance and stability
  • Energy efficiency optimization
  • Terrain adaptation
  • Gait optimization

How It Works

The advanced walking system incorporates these key components:

  1. Dynamic Balance: Continuous adjustment of center of mass and support polygon
  2. Energy Management: Optimization of joint movements and power consumption
  3. Terrain Analysis: Real-time assessment and adaptation to surface conditions
  4. Gait Planning: Generation of optimal walking patterns for different scenarios
  5. Stability Control: Active maintenance of stability during movement
